COVER LETTER

I am eager to work in game development and I am interested in positions related to game development, design, testing, and project management. I have a BSc Hons Computer Games Technology degree(first-class) from Manchester Metropolitan University.
My experience includes rapid prototyping from a 'Game A Week' module during my second year at university. I created and delivered finished games based on given themes every week (examples on my Itch IO profile: https://www.kouda-ha.itch.io).
My dissertation report titled 'Converting a University Teaching Unit into a Computer Game (Gamification)' showcases an educational horror game I developed in Unity. The game was designed around a real-world experience where students questioned the relevance of learning math. In the game, a math-loving ghost teleports these students to a horror version of the university, and they must complete their module to escape it (game link: https://kouda-ha.itch.io/maths, GitHub repository: https://github.com/Kouda-Ha/Maffs). The “final boss” is your end of year exam.
During my third year, I pitched an 'Animal Care Simulator' for 'Advanced Computer Games Design', it was green-lit, and I was made the project manager. This experience made me proficient in Jira and GitHub, overseeing branches, resolving merge conflicts, and merging pull requests into main. The project progressed from zero to a full-fledged vertical slice in just 10 weeks (vertical slice: https://kouda-ha.itch.io/pets, GitHub repository: https://github.com/Level6-ManchesterMet-SCMDT/PetSim).
I am a quick learner and open to exploring new tools and techniques in the computing and gaming domain. Any recommended books or training would be eagerly embraced, as I like to be well-prepared.
Witnessing the evolution of games from design to implementation brings me great joy, fueling my passion for a career in the gaming industry.
I am available to start immediately and would love to discuss how I could be a valuable addition to your team. Feel free to reach out to me via LinkedIn, email (hireme@myself.com), or any preferred method.
