Hello, we're an open-source passion project, acting as an indie dev games studio, community engagement, and wishes to provide educational games development resources in the future.

Hello! I'm known online as 'Kouda_Ha' (he/they) and I made this site to start my passion project of an open-source, indie games dev community.
I want to make something accessible, promoting free education, and something that encourages everyone to be their best and true self, and somewhere you don't have to be scared of trying something new.
This is where Moop comes in. Moop will start on it's journey to be an all inclusive, friendly and welcoming games development environment... at some point! (I'm alone so it's mildly difficult to set up).

Moop possibilities:

  • Game Jams
  • Community Engagement Workshops and Webinars
  • Live-streaming games/demos from indie devs
  • Open-source and volunteering
  • Sharing recommendations of different talent and projects
